Citylife

2018-03-01

This lesson is a class about moving to the big city. I teach in Yangon and every class has a mix of ethnicities and cultural backgrounds, mostly because of urban migration. I’ve had some fun teaching this class which looks at some of the personal and political issues surrounding the topic. I know that sounds really boring but trust me it isn’t and there is even a song by John Denver.
